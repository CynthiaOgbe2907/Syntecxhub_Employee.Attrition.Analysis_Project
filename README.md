# Syntecxhub_Employee.Attrition.Analysis_Project
![HR Analytics Dashboard Screenshot](1001190256.png)

KEY FEATURES & INSIGHTS DEVELOPED:
​Core KPI Tracking: Modeled essential HR metrics including total headcount (1,480), attrition count (238), overall attrition rate (16.08%), alongside demographic baselines like average age, salary, and company tenure.
​Demographic & Behavioral Segmentation: Evaluated attrition distribution across vital dimensions such as gender, marital status, age groups, and business travel frequency.
​Socio-Economic & Academic Analysis: Formulated granular insights  and displaying turnover variance based on education fields and specific salary brackets (Salary Slabs).
​Operational & Role-Based Deep Dives: Structured matrix views and trend lines mapping attrition counts by job roles, specific departments (R&D, Sales, HR), and years at the company to highlight high-risk roles.
​Interactive Data Filtering: Built dynamic, multi-layered slicers (Age Group, Business Travel, Department, Education Field, and Gender) to allow HR stakeholders to customize views and perform real-time exploratory data analysis.

TECH STACK:
Business Intelligence: Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Report Design)
Data Sources: HR Workforce Datasets
